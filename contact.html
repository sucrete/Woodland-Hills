Tee Time Policy

One of the great things about playing golf at Woodland Hills Golf Course is the relaxing casual atmosphere. After all, were are all here at the course to relax and have fun. Part of that relaxed casual atmosphere is our Tee Time Policy. We do not require that you make a tee time. We operate on a first come first served basis. So, if the weather is nice and you and your friends want to come play, simply throw the clubs in the car and head on over to the course. We will be happy to get you on to the course right away. I would however advise either taking a look at our calendar of events or giving us a quick call at 620-223-5060, if you are planning on playing on the weekend due to the fact that a number of our Saturday mornings are reserved for tournaments and outings.
