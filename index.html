Woodland Hills

Woodland Hills is a public 18-hole regulation course 6,156 yards from the Blue Tees, 5490 yards from the White Tees, 5068 yards from the Yellow Tees and 4734 yards from the Red Tees.

Completely renovated as of 2016, Woodland Hills facilities features a new clubhouse with a large back patio. The next time that you are in the Fort Scott area, make sure to bring your clubs and come enjoy one of Southeast Kansas's hidden treasures at the Woodland Hills Golf Course in Fort Scott, Kansas.

Voted one of the top 10 public golf courses in the state by Best Things Kansas 3 years in a row!
