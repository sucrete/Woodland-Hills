The 19th Hole

The 19th Hole Restaurant

The 19th Hole Bar & Grill is located in the Shirkey Golf Course clubhouse. The grill is open to the public, and has great daily lunch specials. Offering a full-range menu, the 19th Hole is a great place to host your luncheon, brunch, birthday party, wedding reception or bridal shower. The convenient, smoke-free location has the potential to suit almost any need.

Call (816) 470-2582 or email info@shirkeygolfcourse.com today to book the private banquet room for your special occasion. Come for the food, enjoy the view.
